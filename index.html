About

I'm an ML Researcher/Engineer currently developing physics-grounded AI agents at Axiomatic AI, an MIT spinout focused on verifiable automated reasoning for scientific discovery. My work spans reinforcement learning, graph neural networks, and large language models, with a particular focus on extrapolative reasoning and robust generalization.

I hold a Master's in Mathematical Engineering from Politecnico di Milano and have conducted research at prestigious institutions including the University of Cambridge and ETH Zurich. My research has been published in top-tier venues and I've been recognized for achieving significant breakthroughs in adaptive mesh methods and multi-agent reinforcement learning.
